About Us

London Academy is a British School in Morocco, accredited by British School Overseas, Council of British International Schools, NCUK and Cambridge International Assessment.

  • London Academy is a British School in Casablanca and Rabat, accredited by the British Department of Education and the Ministry of Foreign Affairs in Morocco. The school provides an enhanced education through the British National Curriculum accredited as a Cambridge International School.
